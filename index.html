About The Blanket Identity

Our mission is to share honest, passionate stories about Asian identity in the U.S. As Asian Americans ourselves, we want the nuances in our identities that are often glazed over in mainstream media to be celebrated instead of hidden. We aim to uplift the voices of Asians in America (whether that means East to South, cultural or racial) and allow them to speak through a series of mini-documentaries.
